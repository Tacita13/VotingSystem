2Fast4U Voting System


The 2Fast4U Voting is a voting web application that helps facilitate operations for meetings. The web application will control questions and keep a rally of the answers.
To use the application:
1. Touch the question you want to do operations on.
2. If you choose old questions, the quesiton with the results and extra information will be displayed.
3. If you choose the current question, you will be presented with the option to vote yes, no or abstained.
To proceed:
1. Touch the login/register option.
2. If you have not registered, choose the register option and enter your information. Later, a code will be sent to your email to confirm it. Once entered, you will be sent to the Home page and you may proceed to use the application.
3. You may not proceed further into the application if you have not confirmed your email.
3. If already registered, once you enter your credentials you will be sent to the Home page and you may proceed to use the application.

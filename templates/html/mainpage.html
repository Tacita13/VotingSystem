2Fast4U Voting System

The 2Fast4U Voting is a voting web application that helps facilitate operations for council meetings. The web application will control questions and keep a rally of the answers. Press the continue option to proceed to login or register. If you have not registered, you will be asked to confirm your email by entering a code sent to your email.
